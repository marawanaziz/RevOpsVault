# Lead Qualification Criteria

## MQL Criteria

### Demographic Scoring
| Signal | Points | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Title match (VP+, Director, C-suite) | +20 | Matches target buyer persona |
| Company size (ICP fit) | +15 | Within ideal employee/revenue range |
| Industry match | +15 | Target vertical/industry |
| Geography match | +10 | Target region or territory |
| Tech stack signal | +10 | Uses complementary technology |

### Behavioral Scoring
| Signal | Points | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Demo request | +30 | High-intent form submission |
| Pricing page visit | +20 | Active evaluation signal |
| Content download (gated) | +15 | Engaged with educational content |
| Webinar attendance | +10 | Participated in live event |
| Multiple page visits (3+) | +10 | Sustained interest |
| Email engagement (opens+clicks) | +5 | Responding to nurture |

**MQL threshold:** 50+ points

## SQL Criteria

### BANT Assessment
| Criteria | Question | Qualified If |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **Budget** | Is there budget allocated? | Budget confirmed or identifiable |
| **Authority** | Can they make or influence the decision? | Decision-maker or strong influencer |
| **Need** | Do they have a problem we solve? | Clear pain point aligned with ICP |
| **Timeline** | When do they need a solution? | Active timeline within 6 months |

**SQL threshold:** Minimum 3 of 4 BANT criteria met

## Disqualification Criteria
- Company below minimum size threshold
- Industry outside target verticals
- No identifiable budget or buying authority
- Explicit disqualifiers from ICP doc (link to ICP)
- Competitor employee or vendor
- Student or personal email with no company affiliation

## Handoff Process
1. Marketing qualifies lead to MQL threshold
2. SDR/BDR validates MQL via outreach (phone, email, LinkedIn)
3. SDR confirms BANT criteria for SQL qualification
4. SQL handoff to AE with qualification summary
5. AE creates deal file in `/sales/deals/`

## SLA
- **MQL response time:** < 4 hours during business hours
- **SDR follow-up attempts:** Minimum 3 touches within 5 business days
- **SQL handoff:** Within 24 hours of SQL qualification
- **AE first contact:** Within 48 hours of receiving SQL
