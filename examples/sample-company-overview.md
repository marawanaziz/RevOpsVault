# Company Overview — Nextera Health

## About
Nextera Health is a Series B health tech company building a patient engagement platform for mid-size hospital systems (200-2,000 beds). Founded in 2021, they've grown to 80 employees with a 12-person sales team. Their platform helps hospitals improve patient communication, reduce no-shows, and increase patient satisfaction scores.

## Industry & Market
- **Industry:** Health Tech / SaaS
- **Business model:** B2B SaaS, annual contracts
- **Stage:** Series B ($22M raised, led by Andreessen Bio Fund)
- **ARR:** $8M (as of Q4 2024)

## Products & Services
- Patient engagement platform (core product)
- Automated appointment reminders (SMS, email, voice)
- Patient portal with self-scheduling
- Provider communication tools
- Analytics dashboard for patient engagement metrics

## Target Market
- Mid-size hospital systems (200-2,000 beds) in the US
- Primary buyers: VP of Patient Experience, CIO, CMO
- Sweet spot: health systems undergoing digital transformation

## Current GTM Motion
- Inbound-led with conference presence (HIMSS, HLTH, ViVE)
- 12 sales reps across 3 geographic territories (East, Central, West)
- SDR team of 4 handling inbound qualification
- Average deal size: $85K ACV
- Sales cycle: 4-6 months (enterprise health system procurement)

## Key Challenges
1. Lead routing is broken — 47-hour average response time on inbound leads
2. SDR-to-AE handoff loses context, causing duplicate discovery
3. HubSpot configuration hasn't scaled with team growth (4 → 12 reps)
4. No clear SLAs or escalation paths for lead management
5. Need to scale from $8M to $15M ARR by end of 2025
